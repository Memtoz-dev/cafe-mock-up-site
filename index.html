About Us

Brief History

This café began as a small dream — just a few friends, a love for coffee, and the hope of creating a space that feels like home. What started with simple ideas and late-night plans has become a cozy corner for conversation, creativity, and comfort.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — to create a space where good coffee, good food, and good people come together. We believe a café should be more than a place to grab a drink; it should be a pause in the day, a moment to connect, reflect, and feel at home.
